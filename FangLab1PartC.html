Ingredients

Cookies

  • 1/2 cup butter
  • 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/2 cup dark brown sugar
  • 1 egg
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 3/4 cups + 1 tbsp all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 tsp baking powder
  • 1/2 tsp baking soda
  • 1 tbsp instant espresso powder
  • 1/2 tsp salt

Cream

  • 3/4 cup mascarpone cheese
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 3/4 cup confectioners sugar
  • 1 tbsp honey
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream
  • 1 tbsp cocoa powder

Instructions

  1. Melt the butter in the microwave and pour it into a large mixing bowl. Let it cool down to room temperature.
  2. Add the granulated sugar and brown sugar and mix.
  3. Add the egg and vanilla extract and mix.
  4. In a seperate bowl, mix flour, baking powder, baking soda, instant espresso powder, and salt.
  5. Mix the dry and wet ingredients all together.
  6. Scoop our 11 cookies and roll them into balls and place them on a pan lined with baking paper.
  7. Leave the cookies to set in the fridge for 1 hour.
  8. Preheat the oven to 355 degrees Fahrenheit.
  9. Bake the cookies for 10-11 minutes.
  10. Let the cookies cool down after baking.
  11. In another mixing bowl, whisk together the mascarpone cheese, vanilla extract, confectioners sugar, honey, and heavy cream with an electric whisk until stiff peaks form.
  12. Using a piping bag, pipe the cream onto the cookies.
  13. Sift cocoa powder on top of the cookies.
  14. EAT!
